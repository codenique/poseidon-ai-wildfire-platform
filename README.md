## Project Objective

To create a visually compelling and high-impact digital experience for an AI-driven environmental monitoring platform that detects early signs of wildfire through real-time social media image analysis.

---

## Use Case

This platform concept demonstrates how AI-based environmental monitoring solutions can be presented to government agencies, environmental organizations, and wildfire response teams.  

The system concept focuses on identifying early warning signals of wildfire risks through intelligent analysis of digital image data, helping authorities respond faster and minimize damage.
